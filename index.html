Discover Wontinola—A Cozy Bowl of Fusion Comfort. Wontinola is where Chinese wontons meets Filipino tinola in the most delicious way. Think juicy, tasty wontons floating in a warm, gingery tinola broth—a beloved comfort soup known for its nourishing, savory, and satisfying flavor. Making it a one of a kind fusion dish that's both familiar and exciting.

Each bowl is handmade with love and care, filled with juicy, flavorful wontons in a soothing tinola broth. It's the kind of meal you crave when you need a break, a boost, or just something really good; A hearty, homey, and perfect for when you need a hug in a bowl.

Wontinola isn't just a meal—it's a cultural story, comfort, and a whole lot of heart in every spoon.
